StudyBuddy CAPS - AI-powered study assistant aligned with South African CAPS curriculum

This software is designed to help students aged 8-18 with their studies through:
- Camera note capture and OCR
- Interactive flashcards
- Mock exam generation
- Progress tracking
- Gamification elements

For support and updates, visit: https://studybuddy.education
